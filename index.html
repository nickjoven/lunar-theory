The Fatal Flaw

Canup's SPH simulations require 70%+ of the Moon to come from the impactor Theia. Apollo samples show Earth and Moon are isotopically identical to parts per million in O, Ti, W, Cr, Si. Every new element tested tightens the match. Physics demands a foreign moon; chemistry demands an identical one.

Side-by-Side: Recession Models

The Moon recedes from Earth as tidal friction transfers angular momentum. The constant-Q model assumes smooth recession; the staircase model predicts lock-stall-unlock events at every rational resonance.

Constant-Q (Standard)

Smooth curve. Predicts LOD = 21.3 hr at 2.45 Gya (observed: 19.0 hr) and 23.5 hr at 620 Mya (observed: 21.5 hr). Overshoots by 9–11%.

Devil's Staircase (This Work)

Step function. Plateaus at resonance crossings. Matches Weeli Wolli (19.0 hr) and Elatina (21.5 hr) rhythmite data on published measurements.

Side-by-Side: Tidal Q

The Moon's tidal quality factor Q varies by an order of magnitude across forcing periods. Standard models need 3–5 parameters. The Stribeck curve uses one functional form with 3 parameters.

Multi-Parameter Rheological Fit

Maxwell, Andrade, or Sundberg-Cooper models. 3–5 free parameters, empirical relaxation timescales. Fits well but no single mechanism.

One Stribeck Curve (This Work)

μ(v) = μk + (μs − μk) exp(-(v/vthr)²). Three parameters. χ²/N = 0.81. Stick regime (long period) → high Q. Slip regime (short period) → low Q.

The One Mechanism

Every row in the table above is an instance of the same transition: an oscillation reaching an Arnold tongue boundary and entering the lower-cost locked regime. The tongue boundaries are saddle-node bifurcations on the Stern-Brocot tree. The coupling is velocity-dependent (Stribeck friction). The framework derives from four irreducible primitives — integers, mediants, fixed points, parabolas — validated in harmonics where the same structure produces the CMB spectral tilt, the Born rule, and general relativity with zero free parameters.

Known weaknesses

  • Mass ratio: 17% residual (largest). Closing requires the full proto-Earth normal-mode spectrum, not just the shell model.
  • Volatile model: fits thermal channel but can't yet separate three concurrent tongue crossings (density, thermal, condensation).
  • Cassini inclination: correctly identifies the mechanism but does not yet compute the exact angle from first principles.

What the data already shows

  • LOD at 2.45 Gya and 620 Mya already favor the staircase over constant-Q on published tidal rhythmite data.
  • Tidal Q fit (χ²/N = 0.81) matches or beats multi-parameter rheological models with fewer parameters.
  • Isotopic identity is forced by fission, not fitted.
